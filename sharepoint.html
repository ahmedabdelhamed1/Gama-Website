SharePoint

Customers use SharePoint to create websites. They can use it as a secure place to store, organize, share, and access information from almost any device. All they need is a web browser, such as Internet Explorer, Chrome, or Firefox. o365-cloud-rain Developers have the ability to design, build, and customize Web sites that run on SharePoint Foundation and SharePoint Server. As a result of using SharePoint, customer will get data-rich Web pages, powerful workflow-enabled solutions, and the look and feel of the site. The created sites can range from small project management team sites to dashboard-driven portal solutions for large enterprises. In-case internet is down then our solutions have the ability to take SharePoint site content offline and collaborate on the content with others. While any team member is offline, he can make changes to SharePoint content that will eventually synchronize back to the SharePoint site..
